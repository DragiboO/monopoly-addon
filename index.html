What is this extension ?

This extension will add some spice to your monopoly game with even more randomness and new mecanisms like stocks and savings, it may need some balances or adjustments.

User Guide

Setup

  • - Enter the names of your players (maximum 8) in the order they play in the game. If you make a mistake, you can delete a player by clicking on their name.
  • - Do the same with the companies (maximum 4).
  • - Enter the value of the highest banknotes in the game in the setup tab, it's really important, make sure it's the right value before starting as this value will be a key for the scaling, and calculations in the game.

Once Launched

  • - The "Action" button allows you to see the available actions during the player or company's turn.
  • - The "Next Turn" button takes you to the next action in the game.

Action Interface

  1. As a player, you can:
    • Invest in your savings to earn an amount every 2 turns.
    • Invest in companies with stocks (maximum 10 per company across all players).
  2. The company's turn:
    • The only action is to spin the wheel to change the company's value.
    3. Buttons:
  3. The "Confirmation" button confirms the game turn. It is mandatory to click it to continue, otherwise, you will be blocked.
  4. The "Close" button closes the action menu and allows you to view different player or company statistics.
    5. Other:
  5. Every few turns, a new interface appears showing the gains of each player, based on their investments in companies and their savings.
